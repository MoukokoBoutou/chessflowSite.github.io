Delete Your Account - ChessFlow

We respect your privacy at ChessFlow. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account directly from the app:

  1. Open the ChessFlow app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete My Account
  4. Confirm deletion

Your account and all associated data will be permanently removed.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your user account
  • Saved openings
  • Comments and related data
  • Any information linked to your profile

Deletion Timeline

Data deletion happens immediately or within a reasonable time after your request.
