Account management

Delete your
account

You can delete your ChessFlow account and all associated data at any time.

How to delete your account

You can delete your account directly from the app:

  1. Open the ChessFlow app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap “Delete my account”.
  4. Confirm deletion.

Your account and all associated data will be permanently removed.

Data that will be deleted

  • Your user account.
  • Your saved openings.
  • Your comments and related data.
  • Any information linked to your profile.

Deletion timeline

Data deletion happens immediately or within a reasonable time after your request.

Need assistance?

If you cannot access your account, contact us at chessflowof@gmail.com.
